Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


Medibot is a model trained on a small dataset of common diseases, it will take the input submitted from the chatbox with the user's symptoms and analyze it to give the most fitting result, along with medications to cure the disease, and a list of other diseases with the same symptoms.

Uncommon diseases or those that requires medical attention would be in the range. For these diseases, it is adviced that the user should seek medical care to be diagnosed correctly.

Medibot's mission is to deliver accessible and fast diagnosis, along with giving advices on how to relieve pain and those of such, and eradicating the hassle of having to take unnecessary visits to the hospital.
